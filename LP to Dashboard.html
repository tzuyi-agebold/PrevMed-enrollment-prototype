Frequently asked questions

What conditions does Bold Care help with?

Bold Care can help with the following conditions: Diabetes, Heart disease or high blood pressure, Obesity and weight management, Arthritis, Emotional support, Cognitive health, and General healthy aging support.

Who is eligible for Bold Care?

You are eligible for Bold if you live in California, Texas, Pennsylvania or Illinois and are an Original Medicare member or a member of one of our supported Medicare Advantage partners such as Aetna, Anthem, Blue Cross, Blue Shield, and UnitedHealthcare. UnitedHealthcare offers $0 copay for in-network telehealth providers, which includes Bold Care Clinicians.

Will I have any co-pay or co-insurance fees associated with my appointments?

Bold Care directly bills Medicare and other insurance plans — no surprise bills. During sign-up, we confirm your eligibility before your first appointment. Most Bold Care patients pay little to nothing out of pocket. Questions? Call us at (424) 577-5266.

Is Bold available where I live?

Bold Care is currently available to members who reside in California, Texas, Pennsylvania or Illinois. Additional states will be added in the coming months.

Does Bold Care offer primary care?

No, Bold is a complement to your primary care provider, not a replacement. Members use Bold Care to receive support in establishing and maintaining lifestyle changes between doctor visits.

Does Bold Care provide prescriptions?

No, Bold Care Clinicians do not prescribe prescriptions. Our members appreciate our root cause approach, which takes a holistic view of who you are and what you need, beyond additional medication.

What is included in my personalized Care Plan?

Your Care Plan includes: activities to manage stress, ways to increase physical activity, strategies to maintain a healthy weight, nutritional guidance, and behaviors to promote restful sleep. Bold Care Plans are grounded in evidence and the latest healthy aging research.

Who are Bold Care Clinicians?

Bold Care Clinicians are board-certified doctors and nurse practitioners who are experts in healthy aging.
